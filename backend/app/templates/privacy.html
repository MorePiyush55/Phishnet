Privacy Policy - PhishNet

Last updated: September 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

PhishNet accesses your Gmail account with read-only permissions to analyze emails for phishing threats. We collect:

  • Email metadata (sender, subject, date)
  • Email content for security analysis
  • Your Google account email and profile information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Detect phishing and malicious emails
  • Provide security alerts and recommendations
  • Improve our threat detection algorithms

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Email access is read-only and encrypted in transit.

4. Data Retention

We do not store your email content permanently. Analysis results may be cached temporarily for performance.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use security APIs (VirusTotal, etc.) to enhance threat detection. No personal data is shared unnecessarily.

6. Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings or our application.
